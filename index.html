Uganda is a landlocked country in East Africa, known as the “Pearl of Africa” for its beautiful landscapes of mountains, lakes, and savannas. Its capital is Kampala, and it has many ethnic groups and languages, with English and Swahili as official languages. Uganda is famous for wildlife like gorillas and lions, and its economy relies on agriculture, tourism, and trade.
