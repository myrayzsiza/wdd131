RiverQuest Adventures

A fun and safe rafting experience for all ages.

Site Name

RiverQuest Adventures – I chose this name because it shows rafting as a safe but exciting quest on rivers.

Optional Domain: riverquestadventures.com

Site Purpose

This site helps people learn about rafting trips, book adventures, and read safety information. It will share trip options, pricing, and tips for first-time rafters.

Scenarios

  • What rafting trips are safe for beginners and families?
  • How do I contact the company to book a trip?

Color Schema

Main Colors:

  • #005f73 – Used for headings, navigation, and accents.
  • #94d2bd – Used for backgrounds and highlights.

Typography

  • Merriweather – For headings
  • Roboto – For body text
